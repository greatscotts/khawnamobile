    Who we are
    Solar Philippines is a project development company completing the country’s first, second, third, and fourth largest ever commercial solar installations in 2014. Bringing together industry veteran engineers and developers, our core team has more than 60 years combined experience and is currently implementing the largest solar project portfolio in the Philippines to date. We are the first local full-service company to handle all of project financing, engineering, construction, and maintenance, and are determined to provide customers the most suitable and reliable system for as little as zero upfront. Underlying our success is that we offer the greatest value as the first local company to achieve economies of scale, fully integrate all services inhouse, and offer aggressive pricing with a long-term perspective. We will complete at least 40 Megawatts in 2014, starting with Central Mall Biñan (0.7MW) by July; SM North Edsa (1.5MW) by October; and Robinsons Palawan (1.2MW) and CityMall Roxas (0.7MW) by November; and in discussion with PureGold, Ayala, San Miguel, and several industrial groups.
